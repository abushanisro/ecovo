Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our EV charging platform

You can book a charging station through our mobile app or website. Simply select your preferred location, choose an available time slot, and confirm your booking. You'll receive a confirmation notification immediately.

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and digital wallets including Apple Pay and Google Pay. You can also set up automatic payments through our app.

Yes, you can cancel your booking free of charge up to 1 hour before your scheduled time. Late cancellations may incur a small fee.

Our stations support Type 2, CCS, and CHAdeMO connectors. You can filter stations by connector type in the app to find compatible charging points.

We provide a 15-minute grace period. After that, your booking may be released to other users. It's best to arrive 5 minutes before your scheduled time.
