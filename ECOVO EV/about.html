Our Mission & Vision

We're committed to accelerating the world's transition to sustainable transportation by making EV charging accessible, convenient, and reliable.

Who We Are

Ecovo was founded in 2024 by a team of passionate entrepreneurs studying at the Institute of Venture Building (IVB), driven by a vision to create sustainable and tech-driven solutions. Recognizing the growing need for eco-friendly innovation, we set out to develop smarter, more efficient systems that bridge technology and sustainability.

What started as an idea has now grown into a pioneering venture dedicated to revolutionizing the way businesses and individuals adopt sustainable practices. Our team is committed to building scalable solutions that make sustainability seamless, accessible, and impactful

Our Purpose

To eliminate range anxiety and make electric vehicle ownership as convenient as traditional vehicles by providing reliable, accessible charging infrastructure nationwide.

Our Commitment

We're committed to maintaining 98% uptime across our network, providing 24/7 customer support, and continuously expanding our charging infrastructure to meet growing demand.
